About me

I'm a Cybersecurity Consultant and the CEO @ Techimpossible, based in Vancouver, BC, Canada. My job is to help businesses close their security gaps and focus on growth.

I write about Cybersecurity, technology and anything in between. I love learning new things and consider myself a polymath.

What i'm doing

  • Cybersecurity Assessments

    I conduct security gap assessments for CIS, NIST, ISO27001, PCI, SOC2 and other frameworks and standards.

  • Security Programs

    I manage 6-12 month security programs for clients that are looking to get ISO 27001 certified or need to get a SOC2 Type 1 and Type 2 attestation.

  • Cybersecurity Mentoring

    If you are interested in mentoring, send me an intro email.
